KS1 - What do people do in forests?

Lots of different people enjoy doing different things in forests. Some people like to play and some people even work in forests! Think about all the different times you have been in a forest. What have you done? It might be...
- Walking
- Riding your bike
- Walking your dog
- Having a picnic
- Riding a horse
- Camping
- Learning
- Playing

Task - take a walk in a forest. Try and find as many people doing these activites as possible!

KS2 - What is climate change?

Climate change describes how our planet's average temperate, rainfall, wind and other weather condition change over a long period of time.

Weather
The daily conditions of the atmosphere over a specific, small local area

Climate
The long-term weather pattern over a large region, area or the globe.

Climate change
Describes how our planet's average temperature, rainfall, wind and other weather conditions change over a long period of time.

Global Warming
Describes the change in the Earth's average temperature over a long period of time.

Task - do you think climate change is good, bad or both? Write a paragraph explaining why.
